About KOBRLA

Collaboratio et Pax per Legem

Our Mission & Vision

Guided by our motto "Collaboratio et Pax per Legem", KOBRLA is established to achieve the following objectives (Article 4 of Articles of Association):

  • Facilitate exchange and networking among members.
  • Protect members' professional legal rights and promote welfare.
  • Share knowledge on UK-Korea legal practices and systems.
  • Provide education to enhance legal expertise.
  • Promote diversity, inclusion, and non-discrimination.
  • Support the Korean community through pro bono activities.
