Membership

Join our network of legal professionals

Membership Types

Full Member (정회원)

  • Korean Attorneys (Byeonhosa)
  • UK Solicitors & Barristers
  • CILEX Fellows
  • Dual Qualified Lawyers

Benefits: Voting rights, eligibility for office, full access to events.

Associate Member (준회원)

  • Foreign Lawyers (involved in UK-Korea work)
  • Patent Attorneys, Accountants
  • Translators/Interpreters in legal field

Benefits: Access to seminars and networking events.

Student Member (학생회원)

  • UK Law Students (LLB, BA Law)
  • GDL, LPC, BPTC, SQE Candidates
  • Korean Law School Students
  • Trainee Solicitors & Pupil Barristers

Benefits: Mentoring, career support, discounted fees.

Institutional Member (기관회원)

  • Law Firms
  • Legal Institutions
  • Related Organizations
